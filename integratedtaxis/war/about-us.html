About Us

Running Taxis started up in 2013, and we’ve been on an epic ride ever since. From our beginnings as an taxi classifieds listing company to launch the first Pro app in 2014, we’ve tried to stay true to our core beliefs— provide cost effective taxis —and to deliver an exceptional experience for our community and subscribers. We owe a huge thanks to our community for joining us on this awesome journey, and we hope that you’ll continue to be a part of our story.
